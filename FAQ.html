Frequently Asked Questions

What type of photography do you take?

My work is described as original and natural. I am particularly interested in gel photography. I also enjoy taking photos of families, pets and flora.

Where will the session be held?

We can either hold the session at my studios or you may choose the perfect location of your choice. My aim is to make you feel comfortable so your photos look natural for years to come.

What is i dont buy any photos?

There is no obligation to purchase your photos and there is no minimum order size.

Can i get digital and hard copy images?

Once we have taken your photos you will receive a number of thumbnails to choose from. These thumbnails will be delivered to your electronically. Once you have decided which photos you would like to purcahse just contact us and place your photo. It's that easy.

What should I wear?

Be yourself, be comfortable and wear a smile.
