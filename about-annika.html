About Annika
Annika was first introduced to photography at
an early age when she was gifted a camera for
her birthday. She initiall developed her passion
for photography by taking photographs of nature
including birds and flora. On leaving school she
continued with this passion as a side activity while
also working full time. Five years ago she decided to
concentrate soley on photography and started her
own business. Over recent years she has developed
her skills in the color gel photography. Annika
particulary loves photography animals, families,
and children and is continually grateful to be
given the oportunity to share her clients lives
and capture their special moments.She has won
multiple awards for her work and continues to
grow and push herself as a photographer.
