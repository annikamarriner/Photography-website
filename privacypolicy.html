Privacy Policy
This privacy policy incorporates information on how Annika Marriner Photography collects, uses, discloses and keeps
secure your personal information

The information collected by Annika Marriner Photography from you assists us in personalising and improving the experience
for our customers. Such information is utilised primarily to ensure supply of goods and services to you the customer.
Such information includes:
- name and address;
- contact phone number;
- email address;

2. Use of personal information
The primary use of personal information is to provide services listed on the website. However, we will use and disclose your
personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act or if required to by law.

3. Disclosure of personal information
Annika Marriner photography may use and disclose your personal information for approved purposes and in a variety of ways
associated with approved purposes.

4. Security and Quality of your personal information
Annika Marriner photography will take resonable steps to ensure that any of your personal information that is collected,
stored, used, or disclosed is accurate, complete and up to date.

5. Access personal information
Annika Marriner Photography shall take resonable steps to limit access to personal information

6. Cookies and other technology
Annika Marriner Photography utilises cookies and other technologies to assist in determining what components of our website
are most popular, and to ensure that our online advertising is bringing customers to our products and services. Cookies may also
be utilised to study traffic patterns and most importantly, to ensure that your experience in interacting with us is convenient.
